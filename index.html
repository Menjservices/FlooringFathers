About Us

Who we serve

We cater to a diverse range of commercial projects, including building contractors, churches, schools, medical facilities, shopfitters, retail stores, and government institutions. Our services also extend to the residential sector, covering new developments, existing homes, and renovations.

What we have to offer

We provide an extensive selection of commercial and residential flooring options, including:

  • Homogeneous Vinyl
  • Heterogeneous Vinyl
  • Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT)
  • RigidCore Products
  • Carpet
  • Carpet Tile
  • Vinyl Sheeting
  • Linoleum
  • Laminate Wood
  • Engineered Wood
  • Artificial Grass

Our Story

With over 25 years of industry experience, Flooring Fathers was established to embody our dedication to superior craftsmanship, exceptional customer service, and innovative flooring solutions. Our extensive knowledge and expertise have driven us to transform spaces and consistently exceed our clients' expectations, ensuring that every project reflects our unwavering commitment to quality and excellence.
